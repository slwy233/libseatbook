# Stop Slop

Use this skill when prose sounds generic, padded, or robotic.

## Workflow
1. Remove throat-clearing and filler.
2. Prefer direct verbs and specific nouns.
3. Cut repetitive structure and mirrored phrasing.
4. Keep the writer's real voice.
5. Tighten until every sentence earns its place.

## Output
- Cleaner rewrite
- Optional short notes on what was removed
